Subscription variant: v2.0
What's unique to subscriptions
No photo hero (cost card instead). Large cost display (EUR 68/mo, EUR 816/yr). Renewal countdown (62 days). Keep/Compare/Cancel decision buttons. "Review checklist" instead of maintenance tasks. Savings-focused "Good to know." No Skip button (subscriptions don't skip).
Cost card
Centrepiece of the subscription screen. Shield icon, large serif amount, period label, annual equivalent, and renewal countdown strip with apricot wash. The cost is the item's identity, not a photo.
Decision buttons
Three equal-width buttons: Keep (sage tint), Compare (lavender tint), Cancel (apricot tint). These are the three things a user can DO with a subscription at renewal time. Not maintenance actions.
Review checklist vs maintenance
Subscription tasks are decision-oriented, not maintenance-oriented. "Review coverage" not "Fix coverage." "Compare alternatives" not "Replace filter." Action labels say "Reviewed" and "Compared" instead of "Done." No Skip button, tasks use "Remind me later" instead.
