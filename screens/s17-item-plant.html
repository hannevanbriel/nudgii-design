What's unique to plants
Taller 260px photo hero (visual condition matters). Health badge overlay (Healthy/Minor issue/Critical). 12-month seasonal care calendar. Photo gallery (horizontal scroll). AI health scan timeline with credit counter. Scan CTA button. No documents section, no warranty.
Seasonal calendar
12 month strip. Active months (March-June, Sep-Oct) get category wash + bold. Current month (April) gets apricot outline. Inactive months are hint-colored. Italic note below: "Active care: March to June, September to October."
Health badge
Frosted glass pill overlaying the photo hero (bottom-left). Shows health dot (sage=healthy, apricot=minor, red=critical) + status text + last scan date. Dark mode uses dark bg instead of light.
Off-season tasks
Tasks outside their season window appear at 55% opacity with hint-colored text. "Protect from frost" shows as November task, clearly greyed out during April. No expand, no actions. Flutter: conditionally hide chevron and disable tap.
Health scans (metered)
Free tier: 3 scans/month. Counter shows "2 of 3 scans used this month." Each scan entry shows health dot, status, date, photo count, and AI-generated description. Scan CTA shows "1 credit" pill.
