Small appliance variant: v2.0
What's unique to small appliances
Consumable product card (affiliate link) inside task expand. Simplified stats row with checkmark "all on track" indicator. No documents grid, no mileage tracker, no countdown cards, no season grouping. Quick DIY tasks only.
Consumable card
Affiliate link moment, shown at task-due time only (inside the refill salt expand). Shopping bag icon, product name, "we may earn a small fee" disclaimer, price pill. Never more prominent than the task itself. One link per task view.
Simplified stats
Three boxes: time owned, total completions (sage), and a checkmark icon for "all on track" (sage wash bg). No countdown cards, no mileage. The checkmark replaces a numeric value when all tasks are within their time window.
No "Why it matters" on simple tasks
Salt refill and filter cleaning are self-explanatory DIY tasks. They get "How to do it" steps only, no "Why it matters" card. "Why" is reserved for tasks where the user might not understand the consequence of skipping.
Task expand: v2.0 pattern
Same shell as boiler/vehicle: numbered steps, task history, Skip/Snooze/Done, Ask nudgii. But no quick info row (no cost, no mileage) and no "Why it matters" for routine tasks. Consumable card appears between steps and history on refill tasks.
