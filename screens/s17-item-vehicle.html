Vehicle variant: v2.0
What's unique to vehicles
Countdown cards (MOT, insurance, seasonal tyres) — the primary block, dated deadlines are what matters. Documents grid (insurance cert, MOT cert, registration). "Good to know" factual reference. Mileage demoted to Details section (2026-04-16 decision: date-based urgency > distance tracking).
Mileage (demoted to Details)
Previously a prominent card below identity. Moved to Details row (tappable, shows "last updated" honestly). UX rationale: updating mileage requires physical friction (walk to car, read dashboard). Users rarely maintain accurate mileage. Date-based deadlines (MOT, insurance) matter more. Mileage-based tasks (oil change) work fine on yearly fallback for 90% of drivers. Contextual prompt may surface inside the oil change task expand when approaching.
Countdown cards
Three side-by-side cards for vehicle-critical deadlines. MOT uses apricot wash (approaching). Insurance shows date. Tyres use sage (seasonal). These are unique to vehicles, no other item type has them.
Documents grid
3-column grid. Vehicle owners keep insurance certificates, MOT results, registration papers. Each card shows icon, name, date. Tappable to view/download. "Add" button for new uploads.
Good to know
Factual reference section. Large value on the left (May, 7 degrees, euro 80), explanation on the right. nudgii's domain knowledge made visible. Builds trust that nudgii knows what it's talking about.
Task expand: v2.0 pattern
Same as boiler: quick info row, "Why it matters" warm card, numbered steps, task history (with mileage notes), Skip (return date) + Snooze + Done, Ask nudgii bar. Oil change history includes km readings. Seasonal task (tyres) has no history stats, just why + how.
