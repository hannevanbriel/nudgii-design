Design direction · editorial warmth

Soft cards. Phosphor icons. Purple pops.

Light mode: every task card carries a soft category fill — warm, lovely, identifiable. The Phosphor icon sits in a rounded container in the card's own color. Purple and apricot are the only vivid things on screen, so they always register as action and personality.

Dark mode: the soft fills give way to a single warm dark surface. The category dot becomes the Phosphor icon — same hue, no circle. More character than a dot, less noise than a filled card.
