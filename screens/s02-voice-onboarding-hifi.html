Shell + navigation
Rule Progress bar: 40%
S-02 is step 2 of 5 = 40% fill. Full-width edge-to-edge, 3px height, outside SafeArea. colorCta #9B7FD4 fill. Track rgba(26,22,18,0.08). Flutter: LinearProgressIndicator inside ClipRRect(borderRadius:100px).
Rule No screen title
There is no heading above the conversation. nudgii's first bubble IS the opening. No "Step X of Y" label anywhere, the progress bar handles that. Back button only.
Rule Back button: arrowLeft SVG
32x32px container, surface bg, 9px border-radius, 0.5px border. SVG: viewBox="0 0 12 12", paths d="M10 6H2" and d="M5 3L2 6L5 9". Minimum tap target wrapped to 44x44px in Flutter with SizedBox.
Conversation bubbles
Rule Sender label
Always lowercase "nudgii", never "NUDGII". No text-transform. 8px DM Sans 500, letter-spacing 0.4px, colorMidAccessible #6B6358. nudgii is always lowercase everywhere: in copy, labels, code, and documentation.
nudgii bubbles
Left-aligned. Surface white bg, 0.5px border rgba(26,22,18,0.08). Border-radius: 12px 12px 12px 4px (bottom-left flat = "tail"). Max-width 88%. Box-shadow 0 1px 3px rgba(26,22,18,0.04).
User bubbles
Right-aligned. colorCta #9B7FD4 bg, colorCtaFg #FAF8FF text. Border-radius: 12px 12px 4px 12px (bottom-right flat). Max-width 84%.
Confirmed chips
Inside nudgii's confirmation bubble. sage-lt #EAF3DE bg, sage #3B6D11 text, checkmark SVG icon 9px. flex-wrap row with 4px gap. Appear immediately after nudgii confirms the item, not in a separate message.
State-specific decisions
Decision No carry-over from S-01
S-01 no longer has interactive items or selection state. nudgii always opens fresh with a discovery question: "What do you have at home? I'll figure out what needs maintenance." No pre-loaded confirmed chips, no "Already added from your selection" message. nudgii guides discovery through conversation.
State 1 Cold-start hint + suggestion chips
"2-3 items is plenty to start." appears as italic hint (11px, colorMidAccessible) below the bubble text. Removes the freeze response from users who think they must add everything. Suggestion chips (dishwasher, boiler, car, lawn) shown below the hint in surface bg, 0.5px border, 100px radius, 8px DM Sans.
State 2 Waveform placement
Waveform appears inside the conversation thread, not overlaid on the input bar. The in-progress transcript fades in at 0.65 opacity as a user bubble. Input bar border becomes rgba(196,80,58,0.30). Mic circle turns colorTerra #C4503A.
State 3 Guided follow-up
After confirming parsed items as sage chips, nudgii guides with category prompts: "Anything else? A car, garden, subscriptions?" One question per turn, max 1 clarifying question. Suggestion chips update to only show items not yet added. Italic em on the question text highlights it as the active ask.
State 4 Continue suggestion
After 2+ turns with items, nudgii gently suggests proceeding. Purple-tinted bubble (rgba(155,127,212,0.06) bg, rgba(155,127,212,0.15) border) visually signals a transition moment. "Ready to review what I found?" in italic colorCta. Two chips: "Continue" (filled CTA, navigates to S-04 Review) and "Add more first" (default chip, keeps conversation open). Never auto-advance. User always chooses.
State 4 Browse fallback link
"or browse instead →" link below the input bar (8px, colorMidAccessible). Lets users switch to S-03 Browse without swiping back. Always visible from State 1 onwards. One tap to change paths, not two gestures. Paths aren't prisons.
Input bar
Rule Scan icon
Four corner-bracket SVG (not a camera). Represents the AI scan entry point. PhosphorIcons.scan in Flutter, 14px, stroke colorMidAccessible stroke-width 1.5.
Rule Privacy note, always visible
9px italic DM Sans, rgba(107,99,88,0.45). Always present below the input bar. Copy: "audio sent for transcription only · deleted after · responds in text". Centers reassurance at the exact moment the user decides to tap the mic.
