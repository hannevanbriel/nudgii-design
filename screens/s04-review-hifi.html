Rule Progress bar
60% fill (step 3 of 5). Full-width, edge-to-edge, outside SafeArea. 3px height.
Entity cards (My Stuff pattern)
Decision Entity cards replace tile grid
Items are shown as entity cards matching the My Stuff tab pattern: 44px icon circle, DM Serif Display name (15px), category wash background, 14px border-radius. This creates visual continuity: items look the same in onboarding review and on the dashboard. Tap to expand shows tasks.
Rule Expanded task rows
Expanding a card reveals nested task rows: category dot on first task, task name (11px), cadence pill (8px), remove X. Pro-locked tasks at 38% opacity with purple "Pro" pill. One entity expanded at a time. 300ms max-height transition.
Rule Remove X
Top-right of each entity card, 22px circle, semi-transparent white bg on category wash. Confirm dialog before removing. Removed items can be re-added via "Add another item."
Free tier + CTA
Rule Pip dots + count
Always visible in sticky bottom bar. 10 pips: filled = plum, empty = light. "X of 10 free items" + "Go Pro" link. Warning state at 8+ items (amber pips).
Rule CTA
"Looks good" with arrow. Full-width pill, plum bg. Proceeds to S-05 AHA + celebration.
Dark mode
Rule Dark tokens
Surface #1C1B18. Plum #B8A0E8. Ink #F0EDE6. Category washes at 10% opacity (was 14% in light). Entity icon circles: rgba(255,255,255,0.08). Tier pips: #B8A0E8 filled. CTA: #B8A0E8 bg, #1C1B18 text.
