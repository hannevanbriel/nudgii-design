Option A: Dot markers, no containers
Good to know
Quick facts
Your boiler heats water and radiators. In Belgium, annual service is legally required. A well-maintained boiler lasts 15-20 years.
Best time to service
Late summer or early autumn. Book in September to avoid the October rush.
Watch out for
Yellow flame, unusual noises, pressure drops below 1 bar, cold spots on radiators.
Typical costs
Annual service EUR 120-180. Filter: EUR 20-40 DIY. Emergency repair: EUR 200+.
