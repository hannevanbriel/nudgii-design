Shell + layout
Rule Progress bar
Full-width, edge-to-edge, outside SafeArea. 3px height, colorCta #9B7FD4 fill at 20% (S-01 = step 1 of 5). Track: rgba(26,22,18,0.08). Never segmented, never dots. Flutter: LinearProgressIndicator inside ClipRRect(borderRadius:100px).
Rule Scroll + sticky zones
Content zone scrolls (ii mark through support line). Button zone is fixed at bottom, outside scroll area. Flutter: Column with Expanded(child: SingleChildScrollView) + fixed bottom Column. Never a Spacer to push buttons down.
Rule ii mark
ii mark: inline SVG, 18x20px. Dark-on-light variant (CTA purple left, plum right, apricot dot). Top-left, no free badge alongside. Free badge removed from this screen (trust line at bottom replaces it).
Content
Headline
DM Serif Display 33px, line-height: 0.97, letter-spacing: -0.55px. "remind itself." in colorCta #9B7FD4 italic (confirmed in dev build). One serif moment for this screen.
Decision Nudge bubbles replace item rows
3 display-only bubbles in nudgii's warm voice replace interactive task rows. Each bubble: category-tinted background at 7% opacity, 12px radius, no border. Staggered rotation (-1.2deg, 0.8deg, -0.5deg) and varied left margins (8px, 32px, 18px) for organic feel. Category icon chip 22x22px. Title 13px DM Sans 500, subtitle 11px italic colorMid. Not interactive: no onTap, no hover, no selection state. Flutter: Column of Container widgets with Transform.rotate. No GestureDetector, no InkWell.
Decision Bubble content in nudgii's voice
Copy is warm and conversational: "Your boiler could use some love" not "Service the boiler." Subtitles frame value: "we'll remind you before winter." Content is locale-dependent and seasonally rotated (stored in database). English shown in design files per design system rules.
Support line
"Nudges when it matters. Silent otherwise." Centered, 13px italic DM Sans, colorMid. Positioned below nudge bubbles with 16px margin-top. One-line value proposition that addresses the #1 objection (notification fatigue).
Button zone
Decision Two paths, clean bottom zone
Path A "Tell me what you have" leads to S-02 (voice/type/scan). Path B "Show me what I can track" leads to S-03 (browse categories). "How do you want to start?" divider removed for cleaner layout. Always vertically stacked, never side-by-side. Sentence case.
Trust line
"Free · no credit card · 2 min setup" below secondary CTA. 11px, colorMid, centered. Replaces the old free badge (top-right) and "Set up in 2 minutes" pill. All three trust signals in one line at the decision point.
Rule Button hierarchy
Path A: colorCta fill, 50px height, pill, shadow. Path B: transparent, 1.5px border, 46px height. Trust line: 11px centered text below buttons. 24px bottom padding for safe area.
