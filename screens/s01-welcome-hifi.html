Welcome

Shell + layout
Rule Progress bar
Full-width, edge-to-edge, outside SafeArea. 3px height, colorCta #9B7FD4 fill at 20% (S-01 = step 1 of 5). Track: rgba(26,22,18,0.08). Never segmented, never dots. Flutter: LinearProgressIndicator inside ClipRRect(borderRadius:100px).
Rule Scroll + sticky zones
Content zone scrolls (ii mark through pivot text). Button zone is fixed at bottom, outside scroll area. Flutter: Column with Expanded(child: SingleChildScrollView) + fixed bottom Column. Never a Spacer to push buttons down.
Rule ii mark
ii mark: inline SVG, 18x20px. Dark-on-light variant (CTA purple left, plum right, plumPale dot). Top-left, no free badge alongside. Trust signals consolidated in bottom trust line.
Content
Headline
DM Serif Display 33px, line-height: 0.97, letter-spacing: -0.55px. "remind itself." in colorCta #9B7FD4 italic. One serif moment for this screen. Below: seasonal chip (garden-tinted bg, 9.5px DM Sans 600).
Decision Photo card carousel replaces nudge bubbles
A swipeable polaroid-style card shows real seasonal photos with handwritten-style captions (Caveat font) demonstrating nudgii's awareness. 3 cards cycle automatically (3.8s interval) or on swipe. Spring card order: tyres, tomato, lawn. Cards auto-advance; swipe left/right to skip. Replaces 3 static nudge bubbles from v2.7.
Polaroid card
White card, 9px padding, slight rotation per card (-1.8°, 2.4°, -1.0°). Shadow: 0 8px 40px rgba(44,40,36,0.22). Photo: 128px tall, object-fit: cover, slight desaturation filter. Caption: Caveat 16px 500, colorInk. Italic emphasis: colorCta. Badge pill: DM Sans 7.5px 700 uppercase, category-tinted background.
Dots + pivot
3 dots below card, 5px diameter. Active: colorCta, scale 1.3×. Inactive: rgba(44,40,36,0.15). Pivot: 2-line italic DM Sans 12px colorMid with bold keyword on line 2. Fades cross-dissolves on card change (220ms). Progress bar advances: 20% → 28% → 36% per card.
Photos (assets)
3 JPEG assets in assets/: s01-tyres.jpg, s01-tomato.jpg, s01-lawn.jpg. Flutter: bundle in assets/images/, declare in pubspec.yaml. Use Image.asset() with BoxFit.cover. Seasonal rotation: swap asset set per season (spring/summer/autumn/winter) in app config.
Button zone
Decision Single CTA: browse only
Path A ("Tell me what you have") removed — too open-ended as a cold start. Single CTA: "Show me what I can track" leads to S-03 (browse categories). Browse gives users a clear starting point: they can see what nudgii tracks before committing to input. Flutter: single ElevatedButton, full width, colorCta fill.
Trust line
"Free · no credit card · 2 min setup" below CTA. 11px, colorMid, centered. All three trust signals at the decision point.
