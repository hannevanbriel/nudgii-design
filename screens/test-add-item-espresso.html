Why it matters

Clean brew group
2 min DIY · weekly
Apr 20
Why it matters
Coffee oils build up and turn rancid after a few days. A weekly rinse keeps the taste clean and prevents mould in the brew chamber.
How to do it
1
Remove the brew group (slides out from the right side panel on the Magnifica)
2
Rinse under warm running water, no soap
3
Let it dry completely before reinserting

Replace water filter
2 min DIY · every 2 months
Jun 13
Why it matters
The filter softens water and removes chlorine. An old filter lets limescale through faster and affects taste. Your De'Longhi uses the DLS C002 cartridge.
How to do it
1
Remove the water tank and take out the old filter cartridge
2
Soak the new filter in water for 5 minutes
3
Insert and run 500ml of water through before first use
Water filter cartridge DLS C002
