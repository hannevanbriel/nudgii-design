The Plan screen.
Editorial timeline. Month by month, task by task. The same voice as the celebration screen — restrained, warm, occasionally funny. Inspired by what works in Residence, adapted to nudgii's system.
Month header — DM Serif Display 26px. Left-aligned. Progress ring shows % of tasks completed that month in purple. Feels like turning a page.
Task row — Date column (day abbr + number), category icon circle in the 3-color system, category label in category color, task name in ink. The same card system as the dashboard, linearized.
Seasonal quote — DM Serif italic, nudgii voice. Appears between months as a natural pause in the scroll. Not every month — only where it fits.
Empty months — Quiet, italic nudge. "Enjoy it while it lasts." Gaps in the schedule feel considered, not broken.
End quote — Large, generous. Like closing a chapter. The app has personality even at the bottom of a list.
