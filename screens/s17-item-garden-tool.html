Garden variant: v2.0
What's unique to garden items
Season-grouped task headers (Spring, Summer, Autumn) with colored dots. No mileage tracker, no countdown cards, no documents grid. Area and grass type in details. "Good to know" section with lawn-specific facts.
Season-grouped tasks
Tasks organized under Spring / Summer / Autumn headers. Each header has an 8px colored dot: spring = cg-bg (garden green), summer = apricot, autumn = mid at 55% opacity. This grouping is unique to seasonal garden items. Vehicles and appliances use flat task lists.
Area specification
Lawn area (~120m²) appears in the meta line and in details. Helps nudgii calibrate task timing: larger lawns take longer to scarify and mow. Future: area-based product recommendations (fertilizer quantity, seed coverage).
Stats row: 2 boxes
Garden items show only 2 stat boxes (tasks this year + active season) instead of the vehicle's 3 countdown cards. Simpler: garden maintenance is seasonal, not deadline-driven.
Good to know
Two factual entries: ideal mowing height (4cm, cg-bg color) and best scarifying month (Apr, apricot). nudgii's domain knowledge made visible. Same component as vehicle variant but with garden-specific content.
Task expand: v2.0 pattern
Same as all variants: "Why it matters" warm card, numbered steps, task history (where applicable), Skip (return date) + Snooze + Done, Ask nudgii bar. Scarify task has full expand with history. Simple tasks (autumn fertilize, last mow) have why + how only.
