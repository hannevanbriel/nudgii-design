Floating pill — specs
Background: white
Border radius: 22px
Padding: 9px 4px 11px
Shadow: 0 4px 20px rgba(26,22,18,0.11)
Bottom position: 14px from screen bottom

The pill floats above content. Content scrolls behind it. It never has a hard edge or background behind it.
FAB — specs
Size: 46×46px
Border radius: 15px — rounded square, not full circle
Background: cta #9B7FD4
Icon: Plus → rotates to × when open
Shadow: 0 4px 16px rgba(155,127,212,0.35)

Rounded square, not circle — matches the pill's language and feels more considered than a typical circular FAB.
Action sheet — 3 paths
Appears from bottom-right, anchored to FAB.
Width: 188px
Cards: white, 16px radius, shadow
Stagger delay: 40/80/120ms
Animation: translateY + scale, spring easing

Same 3 paths as onboarding. Voice → Browse → Search. The same language throughout the whole app lifecycle.
Scrim on open
rgba(26,22,18,0.18) overlay dims the content behind the sheet. Tap scrim or FAB to close. Tab bar dims to 50% opacity when sheet is open — sheet takes full focus.
