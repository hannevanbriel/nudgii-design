Two views of the same information: what users see when choosing a plan, and what the full feature matrix looks like internally. Ultimate is a future tier, not yet priced or designed — included here so decisions made now stay compatible.

What users see — simple benefits
Free

Always free. No credit card.
"The smart reminder list for your home."
  • Up to 10 items — your car, boiler, garden, subscriptions
  • Unlimited tasks per item — nudgii fills in the schedule
  • 3 AI scans per month — photograph an item, nudgii identifies it
  • Weekly digest — a summary of what's coming up
  • For You cards — seasonal tips + item suggestions
  • +2 free items for every friend you invite (up to 20)

"nudgii for the whole household. Predictive, not reactive."
  • Everything in Pro

Coming in v2
Free tier is genuinely useful. nudgii works well with 10 items — most households have fewer. Pro is for people who want the full picture: unlimited items, AI that knows their specific boiler and car, and a shared schedule with their partner. The free tier is not a demo — it's the product.
