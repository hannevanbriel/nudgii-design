why it matters
Skipping the annual check voids most warranty agreements and increases carbon monoxide risk in enclosed spaces. Your warranty expires March 2027 — don't let a missed service void it.
how to do it
Book a certified technician 2-3 weeks ahead. They inspect gas pressure, flue output, and the safety valve. Keep the certificate — your insurer may ask for it.

why it matters
Carbon monoxide is odourless. A working detector is the only warning you'll get. Test the button monthly, replace the unit every 7 years.
how to do it
Press the test button for 5 seconds. If it beeps, you're good. If nothing happens, replace the batteries. If still nothing — replace the detector.
